# Creating exceptional digital experiences through user-centered design.

Hi, I'm Jackson — a UX developer based in Minneapolis with over 7 years of experience leading front-end teams and CMS migrations across healthcare, finance, and nonprofit organizations. I work across the full process: user research, information architecture, interaction design, and front-end development, so I can take a problem from an early sketch through to shipped code.

Lately that's meant migrating Virtuwell's marketing site off static HTML and onto Contentful, taking Open Source North's conference site from WordPress to Strapi and Astro, and leading the front-end for a Fortune 500 finance client's move off a struggling third-party CMS onto Payload. Whatever the industry, the pattern repeats: a content team fighting a CMS that wasn't built for them, and a system that needs someone to migrate the content and rebuild the workflow around it.

My approach is data-driven — I pair what users actually do with what the business needs, rather than guessing at either. That combination is usually what makes an interface feel obvious in hindsight, even though it rarely is in practice.

Outside of work, I'm usually skiing (or wishing I was), trying out a new recipe, or checking out a local brewery.

If you're looking for someone who can take a CMS migration or a digital product from research through to a polished, accessible interface, I'd love to talk.
