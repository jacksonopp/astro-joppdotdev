## Headless CMS Migration

One of the biggest projects I worked on was migrating Virtuwell's marketing pages to a headless CMS — re-architecting the existing pages and building a new front-end to consume the CMS's API. It simplified the content workflow considerably: the marketing team could ship updates without waiting on a developer for every change.

## NGRX Removal

I also removed NGRX from a large portion of one of Virtuwell's applications, which cut load times and made the codebase noticeably easier to maintain.

## Accessibility Improvements

Alongside that, I worked on accessibility improvements across Virtuwell's web applications — keyboard navigation, ARIA attributes, and color contrast fixes — to improve the experience for users with disabilities.

That mix of infrastructure work and accessibility fixes is representative of what most of my time on Virtuwell looked like: less flashy, more foundational.
