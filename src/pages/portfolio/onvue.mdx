# OnVUE

OnVUE is an online proctoring platform that lets people take exams from home. As a UX developer on the team, I worked on a few projects aimed at making the experience less stressful for both candidates and proctors.

## SmartReview Dashboard

One of the main projects I worked on was the **SmartReview Dashboard**, an AI-powered tool that let proctors review and analyze past exams for potential infractions, one test at a time. Building it meant working closely with the AI team to get the human review flow and the model's output talking to each other cleanly.

## A11y improvements

I also worked on bringing the platform in line with WCAG 2.1 — better keyboard navigation, proper form labeling, and improved color contrast — and tested it directly with screen readers rather than relying on automated checks alone.

## Check-in Improvements

The rest of my time went into the exam check-in flow itself: simplifying the setup steps, cleaning up navigation, and adding progress indicators and clearer feedback so candidates knew where they stood before an already stressful exam.

The SmartReview Dashboard was the hardest of the three to get right, and the one I'm most glad we shipped.
